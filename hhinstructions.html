Welcome to Hi, Human. Before you start meeting other users, you need to create your profile. Start by:
1. Choosing your mask. Hi, Human is based on anonymity, so instead of using a profile picture, you will pick a mask.
2. Choose 8 icons that best describe your personality. This will help us learn a bit about you and we can decide which tests to give you.
3. Chose a reason you want to meet people. We will connect you to users who are looking for the same thing as you.
4. Start testing! Anytime you want to connect with a user you must take a short test.
